Who We Are

Born from a Love of the Wild

Adventures Africa was founded in 2010 by a group of passionate Batswana conservationists and guides who believed their country held some of Africa's greatest — and most underappreciated — wilderness experiences.

Over 15 years, we have grown to become one of Botswana's most trusted tourism brands, welcoming thousands of visitors from around the world to discover the magic of the Okavango, Chobe, the Kalahari and beyond.

We operate with a deep commitment to sustainable tourism — ensuring that every safari we run contributes to wildlife conservation and the livelihoods of local communities.

Our Mission

To provide authentic, sustainable and extraordinary safari experiences that connect visitors with Botswana's wild heart — while supporting wildlife conservation and local communities for generations to come.

To be Africa's leading responsible tourism company — inspiring a global love for Botswana's unique landscapes, wildlife and culture, while ensuring they are preserved for future generations to enjoy.
